About Charisma Church Benoni

A vibrant, Spirit-filled church family in Benoni dedicated to encountering God, proclaiming the gospel, and transforming our community.

Our Mission

"To be a thriving church that tirelessly builds God’s kingdom and glorifies God by proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ."

Who We Are

Charisma Church Benoni is a warm, welcoming Pentecostal church located in Northmead, Benoni. We are passionate about authentic worship, powerful preaching of the Word, and building genuine community.

Whether you are new to faith or have been walking with Jesus for years, there is a place for you here.

Spirit-Filled Worship
We believe in encountering the presence of God through passionate worship.
Bible-Based Teaching
Practical, relevant messages that equip you for everyday life.
Genuine Community
Life groups and relationships that help you grow in faith.
